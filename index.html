What is Net Run Rate (NRR)?

Net Run Rate is calculated as: (Runs Scored ÷ Overs Faced) - (Runs Conceded ÷ Overs Bowled)

Enter overs in format: whole overs + balls/6 (e.g., 20.3 means 20 overs and 3 balls)

ICC Rule: If a team is all out, check the "All Out" box. The full quota of overs (20 for T20, 50 for ODI) will be used in NRR calculation, not the actual overs faced.
